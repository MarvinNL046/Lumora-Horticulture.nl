### 4. EU Ecolabel Requirements
**Official EU environmental excellence certification**

**Criteria for Growing Media**:
- Renewable resource content: ≥70% (Paper plugs: 100%)
- Peat content: ≤20% (Paper plugs: 0%)
- Biodegradability: Proven through EN 13432 (Paper plugs: Certified)
- Heavy metal limits: Strict thresholds (Paper plugs: <50% of limits)
- Packaging: Recyclable or compostable (Paper plugs: Fully compostable)

**Benefits of EU Ecolabel**:
- Recognized across all 27 EU member states
- Green public procurement preference
- Consumer trust signal
- Marketing differentiation

**Core Criteria**:
- Peat-free alternatives prioritized
- Compostability certification required
- Carbon footprint documentation mandatory
- FSC or PEFC certification for wood-based products
- Life cycle assessment (LCA) data

- Ban on certain single-use plastics

- Minimum recycled content requirements
- Compostable packaging incentives
