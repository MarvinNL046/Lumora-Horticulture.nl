## SEO Strategy

### Keyword Targeting
- **Primary**: Regional market names + "paperpot" / "paper plug trays"
- **Secondary**: Regional cultivation types, sustainability terms, local regulations
- **Long-tail**: Specific crop types + region, certification + region

### Content Differentiation
Each page emphasizes:
1. **Regional Statistics**: Authentic local market data
2. **Local Regulations**: Country-specific standards and certifications
3. **Regional Trends**: Market-specific developments and priorities
4. **Local Case Studies**: Real examples from that region
5. **Cultural Adaptation**: Language, terminology, business practices

### Internal Linking
- Link to relevant crop-specific pages (tomatoes, herbs, ornamentals)
- Cross-link between regional pages for comparison
- Connect to certification/sustainability pages
- Link to product pages with regional context

## Content Guidelines

### Market Data Requirements
- Use authentic, recent statistics (2023-2024)
- Cite reliable sources (government, industry associations)
- Include regional breakdown where relevant
- Show trends over time (growth, changes, evolution)

### Regional Authenticity
- Use local terminology and measurements
- Reference local brands, organizations, locations
- Understand regional priorities and values
- Adapt tone to local business culture

### Regulatory Compliance
- Accurately represent certification requirements
- Explain how products meet local standards
- Reference specific laws and regulations by name
- Provide compliance evidence and documentation
