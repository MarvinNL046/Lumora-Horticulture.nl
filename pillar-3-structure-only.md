## Implementation Notes

**Design System Compliance:**
- All pages use lumora-cream background
- Hero sections with gradient backgrounds (gold/green/cream mix)
- CTAs: lumora-green-500 (primary), lumora-dark (secondary)
- Tables for data comparison (ROI, costs, savings)
- Highlight boxes with lumora-green-50 or lumora-gold-50 backgrounds
- Progressive disclosure for complex calculations
- Mobile-responsive with stack-on-mobile patterns

**SEO Requirements:**
- 1200-1800 words per page
- H1 with target keyword (<60 chars)
- Meta description with CTA hint (<160 chars)
- 2-3 conversion CTAs per page
- Schema.org Article markup
- Internal linking to related articles (3-5 links)
- Localized URLs per domain (nl/en/de)

**Content Focus:**
- Business-focused (B2B professional tone)
- Data-driven with concrete numbers
- ROI calculations and cost-benefit analyses
- Real-world examples and case studies
- Practical implementation guidance
- Professional credibility (no hype, just facts)
